# Generation Feedback Loop

## Purpose

Define the bounded iterative generation cycle for drafting against v3 through v6.1 objectives without creating an uncontrolled self-improving loop.

## Loop

1. Generate draft
2. Evaluate draft
3. Identify weakest dimensions
4. Revise only against allowed targets
5. Re-evaluate
6. Stop when:
   - hard blockers are cleared and bounded objectives are met
   - no meaningful improvement remains inside the allowed pass budget
   - maximum pass count is reached

## Hard Rules

- v4 failure blocks promotion
- v6 cannot override v4 or v5
- viral scoring cannot justify shallow content
- insight density must remain above threshold
- no pass may mutate validators, thresholds, libraries, or runtime rules

## Allowed Revision Priorities

- repair structural obligations first
- then repair meaning quality and redundancy risk
- then improve reader impact
- then improve viral expression
- then refine platform fit as contextual output only

## Revision Discipline

- revise against the fewest necessary weak dimensions
- preserve already-strong dimensions where possible
- do not trade insight density for higher hook or arousal
- do not trade v4 stability for v6 growth signals

## Stop Conditions

- immediate stop on unresolved v4 blocker at final pass
- immediate stop if revision introduces generic motivation or shallow virality
- bounded stop after configured revision budget
